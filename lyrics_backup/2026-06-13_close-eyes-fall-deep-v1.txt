[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Close your eyes and fall into the deep,
Where the waking world is fast asleep.
I can feel your pulse against my skin,
This is exactly where we both begin.

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers on a quiet road,
We left the rushing for a slower mode.
Your fingers trace a map across my hand,
A silent language I can understand.

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers glow inside the open space,
I see the firelight upon your face.
The air is heavy and the room is warm,
I want to be your shelter in the storm.

[Chorus]
So let the rain come down on the tin roof top,
When I pull you closer, I don't want to stop.
Close your eyes and fall into the deep,
You’re the only promise that I want to keep.

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, fall into the deep with me,
Just fall into the deep with me.

[Verse 2]
The shadows dance upon the wooden floor,
I’ve never wanted anybody more.
The way you breathe is like a gentle breeze,
You bring a restless wanderer to his knees.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The embers glow inside the open space,
I see the firelight upon your face.
The time is fading but the night is ours,
We’ll trace the ceiling like a field of stars.

[Chorus]
So let the rain come down on the tin roof top,
When I pull you closer, I don't want to stop.
Close your eyes and fall into the deep,
You’re the only promise that I want to keep.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, fall into the deep with me,
Just fall into the deep with me.

[Bridge]
Close your eyes and fall into the deep,
Where the quiet moments are ours to keep.
Every heartbeat whispers what we need,
This is an endless promise from Saeed.
Hold on tight and let the feeling rise,
We found a forever in the midnight skies.

[Spoken]
There is nowhere else I'd rather be.
Just stay right here with me.

[Final Chorus]
So let the rain come down on the tin roof top,
When I pull you closer, I don't want to stop.
Close your eyes and fall into the deep,
You’re the only promise that I want to keep.
